Managing accessibility in digital projects

Need some help plotting your course to create accessible digital products? In this course, we cover how accessibility decisions need to be factored into all aspects of planning, delivery and launch of your product.

What you‘ll learn

  • Impacts of accessibility on users‘ experience
  • Legal requirements for your organisation
  • Guidelines and standards including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) and procurement
  • Incorporating accessibility throughout the project lifecycle
  • Technology choices
  • Communications and ongoing maintenance

Who should attend

  • Project Managers
  • Business Analysts
  • Product Owners
  • Digital Professionals

Knowledge level

No prior accessibility knowledge is needed.
