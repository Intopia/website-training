Managing accessibility in digital projects

Need help plotting a course towards accessible digital products? Explore how accessibility decisions can be factored into all aspects of planning, delivery and launch of your product.

What you’ll learn

  • Impacts of accessibility on user experience
  • Legal requirements for your organisation
  • Guidelines and standards including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) and procurement
  • Incorporating accessibility throughout the project lifecycle
  • Technology choices
  • Communications and ongoing maintenance

Who should attend

  • Project Managers
  • Business Analysts
  • Product Owners
  • Digital Professionals

Knowledge level

No prior accessibility knowledge is required.

Technology requirements

To take part in some of the activities, you’ll need to download and install the following browser extensions before the start of the course:

  • axe DevTools (Chrome, Firefox or Edge)
  • Accessibility Insights for Web (Chrome or Edge)
  • Wave Evaluation Tool (Chrome, Firefox or Edge)

If there’s anything that stops you from installing these, let us know as soon as possible and we can explore other options for you.
