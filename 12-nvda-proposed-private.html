Accessibility testing with NVDA

Do you want your beautiful HTML emails to be available and accessible to everyone? This course explores how to write, design and mark-up accessible emails.

What you’ll learn

  • best practices for email content
  • accessible links and subject lines
  • accessible use of colour
  • accessible layouts, images and multimedia
  • accessible HTML markup for emails
  • testing HTML emails for accessibility

Who should attend

  • Email content creators
  • Email designers
  • Email developers
  • QA testers

Knowledge level

No prior knowledge of accessibility is required.
