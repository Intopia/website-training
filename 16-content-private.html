Creating accessible content – all session options

Intopia offers a range of optional sessions for aspects of accessible content creation. You can pick any of the following sessions to create training that suits your exact needs. Sessions include:

  • Session 1: Core writing practices
  • Session 2: Accessible content types
  • Optional session 3: Accessible UX content
  • Optional session 4: Introduction to accessible documents (Word/Office and PDF)
  • Optional session 5: Accessible PDF remediation
  • Optional session 6: Accessible multimedia content
  • Optional session 7: Accessible social media content
  • Optional session 8: Accessible InDesign

Session 1: Core writing practices

Explore essential writing best practices, inclusive language techniques, and how to craft content that connects with everyone, ensuring your message reaches its fullest potential.

Duration

  • 1 x 2-hour session

This session covers:

  • Writing best practice
  • Plain language
  • Inclusive language
  • Links

Cost: AUD $2,000 (excluding GST) for up to 20 people.

Optional session 4: Introduction to accessible documents (Word/Office and PDF)

Learn to enhance readability, structure, and navigation in Word and other MS Office documents. Master essential techniques such as optimizing content layout, utilizing built in accessibility features, and seamless exporting into accessible PDFs.

Duration

  • 1 x 2-hour session

This session covers:

  • Making your Word and PowerPoint document readable
  • Structuring your documents
  • Content order and layout
  • Navigation and document properties
  • Checking Accessibility
  • Templates and exporting
  • How to make accessible PDFs

Cost: AUD $2,000 (excluding GST) for up to 20 people.
