Creating accessible content – all session options

Session 1: Core writing practices

Explore essential writing best practices, inclusive language techniques, and how to craft content that connects with everyone, ensuring your message reaches its fullest potential.

Duration

  • 1 x 2-hour session

This session covers:

  • Writing best practice
  • Language
  • Links

Session 2: Accessible content types

Discover how to optimize images, headings, and lists to ensure seamless navigation and engagement for all users, empowering your message to resonate across diverse audiences.

Duration

  • 1 x 2-hour session

This session covers:

  • Images
  • Headings
  • Lists
  • Page titles
  • Tables
  • Colour

Optional session 3: Accessible UX content

From creating clear labels and form instructions to preventing errors and delivering dynamic messaging, learn how to design interfaces that prioritize inclusivity and enhance user satisfaction.

Duration

  • 1 x 2-hour session

This session covers:

  • Labels
  • Instructions
  • Errors
  • Error prevention
  • Dynamic messaging

Optional session 4: Creating accessible Word documents

Learn to enhance readability, structure, and navigation in Word documents, mastering essential techniques such as optimizing content order, utilizing templates, and seamless exporting for inclusive user experiences.

Duration

  • 1 x 1-hour session

This session covers:

  • Making your Word document readable
  • Structuring your Word document
  • Content order and layout
  • Navigation and document properties
  • Checking Accessibility
  • Templates and exporting

Optional session 5: Creating accessible PDF documents

Gain invaluable skills in tagging content, optimizing lists and tables, managing focus and reading order, setting document properties, and troubleshooting accessibility barriers, ensuring your content reaches every audience effectively.

Duration

  • 1 x 2-hour session

This session covers:

  • Tagging content
  • Accessible lists and tables
  • Focus and reading order
  • Document properties
  • Checks

Optional session 6: Accessible multimedia content

Explore the essentials of captions, transcripts, audio descriptions, and sign language integration, ensuring your content resonates inclusively with all audiences.

Duration

This session covers:

  • Sign language
  • Audio and visual considerations
