Creating accessible content for social media and multimedia

Want your content to connect with a broader community? Explore how to make your written, visual, and media content accessible no matter what platform you’re publishing to.

What you’ll learn

  • Structuring content for easy scanning
  • Content that is easy to read and understand
  • Using inclusive language
  • Accessible alternatives for images
  • Media alternatives for audio and video
  • Creating accessible posts for social platforms

Who should attend

  • Content writers and editors
  • Publishers
  • Social media creators
  • Multimedia creators

Knowledge level

No prior accessibility knowledge is required.

Technology requirements

We use online tools such as FigJam to work together during this course. You won’t need to install or create an account to use these tools.
