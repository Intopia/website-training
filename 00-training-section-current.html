Accessibility training

Level up your accessibility skills and have fun while you do it.

Accessibility training will teach you how to create accessible experiences. Our courses give you accessibility knowledge and tools tailored to your role and your everyday work. Our instructors have years of real-world experience and create lively, interactive environments for learning.

What’s great about Intopia training?

  • Role-based training for everyone. Everyone has a role in building accessible experiences. Our training courses are for everyone from managers, researchers, designers, engineers, testers, and content writers.
  • Active learning. We don’t do boring lectures. Actively participate in your own learning with amusing and exciting activities, quizzes, and discussions. Then, put what you learn into practice straight away.
  • Experienced trainers. Our trainers know their stuff. They’ll share their real-life stories and examples of working with organisations and people who benefit from accessible experiences.

Training for your organisation

If you want training for a group of people in your organisation, this is for you. We can help you decide if you need:

  • one or more of our standard courses
  • a completely custom training program, or
  • something in between.

We can run these training courses online or in-person, according to your need.
