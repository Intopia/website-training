Writing accessibility requirements

Need some guidance including accessibility requirements in your projects? Explore how to include accessibility into processes such as the definition of ready, the definition of done, and user stories and acceptance criteria.

What you’ll learn

  • How people with disabilities use digital technology
  • Defining accessibility requirements
  • Writing accessibility acceptance criteria
  • Including accessibility in your definition of done, user stories and acceptance criteria
  • Applying accessibility requirements to your acceptance criteria

Who should attend

  • Product owners
  • Business analysts
  • Strategists
  • Anyone who writes requirements

Knowledge level

No prior accessibility knowledge is required.

Technology requirements

We use online tools such as FigJam to work together during this course. You won’t need to install or create an account to use these tools.
