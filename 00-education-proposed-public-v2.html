Ask us about Education and Learning

Our Education and Learning services offer on-demand and instructor-led training options to enhance your team’s accessibility and inclusion skills. Our training is role-based and designed to meet different learning styles. Whether for designers, engineers, marketers, managers, or *insert other role here*, our training gives your team the skills to tackle accessibility and inclusion with confidence.

Intopia’s trainers are experienced professionals who share valuable insights and stories, providing real-world practical examples. This ensures that our training is relatable and grounded in real industry experiences.

Our training approach is interactive, with engaging activities, quizzes, and discussions. This facilitates active participation and immediate application of concepts, making learning both effective and enjoyable.

Our online training platform, Intopia Learn, offers free and paid on-demand courses in digital accessibility and inclusion. The platform is flexible and allows participants to engage with the material at their own pace. This creates a learning environment that accommodates different schedules and learning styles.
